About Me

UX/UI designer from the TravelTech world who is fluent in photoshop and puns.

I am a hands on problem solver who really loves designing cool products. I have created marketing advertisements, email promotions, crowdfunding campaigns, and even a white noise fan app that will be fully launched later this month (I’ll give you a sneak peak if you ask!).

What excited me about UX/UI is that you always have to be one step ahead - designing a layout predicated on understanding the consumer and how they will interact with your product. What is a main indicator that you have a successful and sustainable application? Having the user enjoy using it!
